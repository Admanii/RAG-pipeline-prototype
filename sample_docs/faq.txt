Q: How can I reset my password?
A: You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address to create a new password.

Q: Can I change my email address?
A: Yes, you can update your email address from your Account Settings page. You'll need to confirm the new email through a verification link.

Q: What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?
A: Immediately reset your password and contact our support team. We also recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for added protection.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, AMEX), PayPal, and Stripe.

Q: Can I update my billing details?
A: Yes, go to Billing > Payment Methods in your account settings to update or add a new payment method.

Q: Will I receive an invoice?
A: Invoices are automatically emailed after every successful payment and are also available in your Billing History.

Q: What is your refund policy?
A: Refunds are processed within 7 business days upon request and approval. Please contact support within 14 days of your purchase.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime from your Subscription Settings. Your access will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.

Q: Will I lose my data if I cancel?
A: Your data will be stored for 30 days after cancellation. You can export it before it is deleted.

Q: How do I upload documents for analysis?
A: Navigate to the Upload Center, drag and drop your files, or use the bulk import feature.

Q: What file formats are supported?
A: We currently support .txt, .pdf, .md, .docx, and .csv formats.

Q: Is there a size limit on documents?
A: Individual files must be under 25MB. Contact support for larger file handling.

Q: What is RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)?
A: RAG combines document retrieval with a language model to generate more accurate and grounded responses.

Q: What models are supported?
A: OpenAI GPT-3.5/4, Azure OpenAI, and GitHub-hosted LLMs via Azure-compatible endpoints.

Q: Where are my documents stored?
A: In a Pinecone Vector Database hosted in AWS US-East-1. Raw text is not stored unless explicitly enabled.

A: Email support@yourdomain.com or use the Help Center on our website.
